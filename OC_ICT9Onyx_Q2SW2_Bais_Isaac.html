Golden Retriver One of the most popular breed in America. They are serious in hunting and field work, They can be trained as guides for blind people, and even in search-and-rescue teams. They are medium sized and have a gold coat that gives them their name.
Boston Terrier Are small dogs with short tails, and well balance, and have a nice tuxedo jacket coat to them. They have big round eys and have good manners earning him the nickname "The American Gentlmen."
German Shepherd One of the most famous and recognizble breeds in the world. The Germans Shepard are good all-purpos workers. They are large, agile, muscular dogs who are even highly intelligent. We can even see them trained for police work to be police dogs.
Labrador Retriver This is America's most popular dog breed. They are friendly, and high-spirited dogs. They are also very tall depending on their gender and come in yellow, black, or chocolate coats.
Siberian Husky One of my personal favorite dog breeds, the Siberian Husky are medium sized dogs who have a thick coat of fur and are very friendly . More suitable for cold environments and have great endurcance. they can pull light loads at moderate speeds in a frozen environment.
American Bully The American Bully is a mix bread and are very intimidating. But do not let their appearance distract you from their freindly nature. They are usually energetic and playful, and from personal experience I can say they are freindly and very energetic.
Irish Setter They are gundogs who are high-spirited known for their grace and swiftness. They have a very nice red coat. And are known as good family dogs, and are very beutiful.
Greyhound Is the fastest dog out of all breeds, Greyhounds are usually gentle,noble, and sweet-temperd. They are very slim and tall dogs, with slim and narrow head. But with that very slim nature on its body comes with great speed.
Shich Tzu These little dogs are very recognizble. They have big dark eyes and are very furry. Some have varying in length for their fur.
Shiba Inu Is a Japanese breed and is a very old. The Shiba Inu is little but very well muscled. It is the most popular dog breed in Japan.
